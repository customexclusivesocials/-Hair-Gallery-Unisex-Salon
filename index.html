Our story

What your hair says about you, we help you say well.

How we style our hair says a lot about us — traditional or contemporary, reserved or outgoing. If you're tired of the same old look and don't know where to take it, our stylists are ready to help.

For over two decades we've kept up with the latest cuts, colour and techniques, serving loyal regulars and walk-ins alike at honest, affordable prices.

We welcome every hair type — long or short, thick or thin, straight or curly — for adults, seniors, kids and teens. Weddings, proms, parties, baby showers and everyday days included.
