About Us

Located on Main Street, just steps from the world-famous Fallsview tourist district, The Hair Gallery Salon & Spa delivers exceptional results in a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Whether you are a local resident or a visitor looking for a fresh style during your stay, our experienced, licensed stylists are here to help.
